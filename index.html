About me

Customer service taught me
exactly what a SOC needs.

I'm Myles Tuazon, a Customer Service Representative with 3–4 years of experience based in the Philippines who's making a deliberate, well-planned shift into cybersecurity. I'm currently halfway through the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate on Coursera, and I've been building hands-on skills in threat analysis, network security, and risk management since day one.

I've always been the "techy person" in the room — the one who edits HTML just to see what breaks, inspects page elements out of curiosity, and figures out software by exploring it. I'd rate my technical comfort at a solid 7/10: I'm not a developer, but I pick up technical concepts fast and I'm not afraid to go deep.

My customer service background isn't a gap — it's an advantage. SOC teams don't just need people who can read logs; they need people who can communicate incidents clearly, stay composed during high-pressure situations, and write reports that non-technical stakeholders actually understand. That's exactly what I've been doing for years.

I'm targeting Tier 1 SOC Analyst roles where I can contribute to threat monitoring and incident triage. I bring curiosity, discipline, and a genuine drive to protect systems and the people who use them.
